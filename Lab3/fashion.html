Fit Checks and More

Fashion has been a part of my life since I was basically born. My mom owns her own clothing consignment boutique and has owned it for 21 years! I remember all the memories of me working the front desk or helping out customers. I could barely see over the counter!

Today, you will rarely see me wear sweatpants ever as well as me telling you my go-to phrase: "look good,feel good". This mantra is something that I think is essential for every woman out there. We all have those days where we feel absolutely awful. You put on a cute fit and slap on some makeup, you can't tell me you don't feel like a new woman after that! I have started using my social media, to showcase some of these OOTD's and to be honest, I don't think I will be able to stop.

Why will I not be able to stop?

  • The confidence I get is unmatched!
  • It encourages others to post and share ideas!
  • I love giving girls some inspo everyday!
  • Interacting with my people is something that I love to do everyday!
  • It's a thing that I enjoy to do!

So next time you have an outfit that deserves a moment, post it! You won't regret it!
