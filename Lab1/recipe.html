Fruit Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 roll of Sugar Cookie Dough
  • 1 package of cream cheese
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 2 kiwi fruits cut up in slices
  • 1 cup of halved strawberries
  • 1 cup of blueberries

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F. Spray 12-inch pizza pan with cooking spray. Break up cookie dough in pan; press dough evenly in bottom of pan to within 1/2 inch of edge.
  2. Bake 16 to 20 minutes or until golden brown. Cool completely on cooling rack, about 30 minutes.
  3. In small bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla with electric mixer on medium speed until fluffy. Spread mixture over cooled crust.
  4. Arrange fruit over cream cheese. To serve, cut into wedges or squares.
  5. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour.
  6. Cover and refrigerate any remaining pizza.
